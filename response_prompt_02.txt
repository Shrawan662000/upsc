### Environment & Ecology

Question: Consider the following statements regarding the UNESCO State of Ocean Report, 2024:

Statement-I: The report indicates significant ocean warming and rising sea levels.
Statement-II: The report aligns with the UN Decade of Ocean Science (2021–2030) and highlights issues like deoxygenation and acidification.

Which one of the following is correct in respect of the above statements?

a) Both Statement-I and Statement-II are correct and Statement-II is the correct explanation for Statement-I  
b) Both Statement-I and Statement-II are correct and Statement-II is not the correct explanation for Statement-I  
c) Statement-I is correct but Statement-II is incorrect  
d) Statement-I is incorrect but Statement-II is correct  

Answer: a) Both Statement-I and Statement-II are correct and Statement-II is the correct explanation for Statement-I  
Explanation: Both statements accurately reflect the findings of the UNESCO State of Ocean Report, which discusses ocean warming, rising sea levels, and aligns with the UN Decade of Ocean Science.

### Indian Geography

Question: Which of the following statements are correct regarding the Maharani Temple in Gulmarg?

1. It was built in 1915 by Mohini Bai Sisodia.
2. The temple is dedicated to Lord Vishnu and Goddess Lakshmi.
3. It showcases Indian-Persian architectural style.
4. The temple was restored by the Indian Army in 2021.

Select the answer using the code given below:
a) 1 and 2 only  
b) 1 and 3 only  
c) 2, 3 and 4 only  
d) 1, 3 and 4 only  

Answer: d) 1, 3 and 4 only  
Explanation: The Maharani Temple was indeed built in 1915 by Mohini Bai Sisodia, is dedicated to Lord Shiva and Parvati (not Vishnu and Lakshmi), showcases Indian-Persian architecture, and was restored by the Indian Army in 2021.
